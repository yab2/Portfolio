===========================================
HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES:
===========================================

1. RESEARCH FIRST:
   - Check their LinkedIn profile
   - Read recent posts/comments
   - Find common connections
   - Note their role and tenure

2. PERSONALIZE:
   - Use their first name
   - Reference something specific (post, project, company news)
   - Show genuine interest

3. TIMING:
   - Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM - 11 AM (highest response rate)
   - Follow up after 5-7 days if no response
   - Don't send on weekends

4. FOLLOW-UP (if no response after 1 week):
   "Hi [Name], following up on my message from last week. I'm still very interested in [opportunity]. Would you have time for a quick chat this week?"

5. AFTER POSITIVE RESPONSE:
   "Thank you for the response! I'd love to learn more. Are you available for a 15-minute call on [Day] at [Time]? Happy to work around your schedule."

===========================================
RESPONSE RATE BOOSTERS:
===========================================

1. Add LinkedIn Premium (1 month free trial)
   - "Featured" badge shows you're serious
   - InMail credits for messaging anyone
   - See who viewed your profile

2. Engage BEFORE messaging:
   - Like 2-3 of their posts
   - Comment thoughtfully on their content
   - They'll recognize your name when you message

3. Keep it SHORT:
   - Under 300 characters = 10x more responses
   - Get to the point immediately
   - Make it easy to say "yes"

4. Include portfolio link:
   - Shows you're prepared
   - Demonstrates initiative
   - Easy to share with hiring team
